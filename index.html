Trust is one of the biggest challenges in the industry.

Customers can't see what you see in the workshop or in the field.

When faults are explained verbally, many customers hesitate to approve work because they don't fully understand the issue.

InspectEquip removes that barrier by giving mechanics a simple way to document inspections and show clear proof of what they find.

Instead of telling customers what's wrong — you show them.

Built by Field Mechanics

InspectEquip was created by field mechanics who were tired of trying to hold pens with greasy hands and handing customers dirty, often hard-to-read paperwork.

We needed a better way to document inspections, capture photos, and clearly show customers what we were seeing in the workshop and in the field.

So we built InspectEquip to solve the problem in our own workflow.

Now we're giving other mechanics and workshops the opportunity to solve the same problem in theirs.

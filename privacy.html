InspectEquip Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 — Version 1.1

InspectEquip™ ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is a software application operated by Vortech Mechanical Services Pty Ltd (ACN 655 628 050), an Australian registered company.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the InspectEquip mobile application.

1. Information We Handle

InspectEquip is designed primarily as an offline-first application. Information entered into the App is generally stored locally on your device and used to provide the App’s core functionality.

Depending on how you use the App, information handled may include:

  • company and business details;
  • customer information entered by you;
  • vehicle, machinery, and equipment details;
  • inspection notes, photos, and reports;
  • other user-generated inspection content;
  • license keys, subscription identifiers, and subscription status information;
  • device identifiers and device details associated with a subscription; and
  • recovery email address and recovery email verification status, where provided by you.

We do not intentionally collect personal information beyond what is reasonably required for the App to function.

2. How Information Is Used

Information handled by the App is used to:

  • create and manage inspection records;
  • generate reports, PDFs, and related documents;
  • provide core application functionality;
  • maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the reliability of the App;
  • collect limited technical diagnostic data to improve app stability;
  • support subscription, license verification, device registration, and recovery features; and
  • respond to support, help, and feedback requests submitted by you.

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

3. Subscription and Recovery Information

InspectEquip uses a license-based system rather than user accounts.

If you activate a subscription or license within the App, the following information may be stored and processed:

  • license key and subscription identifier;
  • device identifiers associated with the subscription;
  • device details such as device name, model, and platform;
  • recovery email address, if provided by you; and
  • recovery email verification status and related verification request data.

The recovery email address is used solely for:

  • verifying ownership of a subscription;
  • enabling subscription recovery and management of registered devices; and
  • authorising sensitive actions such as transferring or recovering access.

Recovery email addresses are not used for marketing or promotional purposes.

We do not use this information to track users outside of subscription and access control functionality.

4. Data Storage

Inspection data is primarily stored locally on your device and/or within storage services selected, configured, or controlled by you.

Vortech Mechanical Services Pty Ltd does not routinely store inspection data on company-operated servers.

Limited subscription, device registration, recovery, and support-related information may be stored using third-party infrastructure and platform services required for app functionality.

You are responsible for:

  • managing backups;
  • maintaining appropriate data retention practices;
  • securing your device and any storage locations used by you; and
  • complying with any privacy, record-keeping, or legal obligations applicable to your business.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose your data except:

  • where required by law;
  • to comply with legal obligations;
  • where required to provide subscription, billing, email delivery, app distribution, diagnostic, or infrastructure services;
  • where required to support Apple App Store or Google Play functionality; or
  • where you choose to export, transmit, or share data yourself.

6. App Store, Billing, and Third-Party Services

If you purchase or manage a subscription through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, billing and subscription-related information may be collected and processed by those platform providers in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

InspectEquip may rely on third-party platform or device services that support app functionality, security, storage, distribution, diagnostics, email delivery, and subscription or licence verification.

These may include services used for crash reporting, infrastructure hosting, cloud functions, subscription verification, and support or feedback email delivery. We do not control how those third-party services handle information outside the App.

7. Diagnostics and Error Reporting

InspectEquip may collect limited technical diagnostic data to help identify errors, improve performance, and enhance the reliability of the App.

This may include:

  • crash reports and error logs;
  • device type, operating system version, and app version;
  • technical information related to app performance and failures; and
  • limited device or subscription-related context needed to troubleshoot issues.

This diagnostic data does not intentionally include inspection report content, photos, customer details, or operational content unless such details are manually included by the user in a support or feedback request.

Where possible, diagnostic data is minimised and used solely for troubleshooting, maintenance, and improving the App. It is not used for marketing or cross-service tracking purposes.

8. Feedback and Support Requests

If you contact us through the in-app feedback form, we may collect and process:

  • your name and email address;
  • the message or request you submit;
  • optional subscription and registered device details, if you choose to include them; and
  • technical context related to the request.

This information is used solely to:

  • respond to your enquiry;
  • provide support; and
  • improve the App.

Feedback submissions may be transmitted and processed through third-party email delivery providers solely for the purpose of message delivery and support handling.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to help protect information processed within the App. InspectEquip may provide optional security features, such as PIN access control, to assist in restricting unauthorised access.

Security also depends on the security of your mobile device, operating system, user permissions, and any storage solutions you use. You are responsible for securing your device, enabling device-level passcodes or biometric protection where available, and controlling physical access.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. International Use

InspectEquip may be used internationally. By using the App, you acknowledge that information entered into the App may be stored on devices or services located in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, depending on how you use the App and any services selected by you.

11. Future Features

We may introduce additional features in future versions of the App, including cloud sync, analytics, backups, integrations, in-app billing, or other online services. If we do, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect how those features handle information.

12. Children’s Privacy

InspectEquip is a professional business tool and is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

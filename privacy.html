InspectEquip Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

InspectEquip ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is a software application operated by Vortech Mechanical Services Pty Ltd (ACN 655 628 050), an Australian registered company.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the InspectEquip mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly when using the App, including but not limited to:

  • Company and business details
  • Customer information entered by you
  • Vehicle and machinery details
  • Inspection notes, photos, and reports
  • User-generated inspection content

InspectEquip does not intentionally collect personal information beyond what is required to operate the App’s functionality.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Create inspection reports
  • Generate PDFs and records
  • Provide core application functionality
  • Improve reliability and performance of the App

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

3. Data Storage

Inspection data is primarily stored locally on your device and/or within storage services selected or configured by you.

Vortech Mechanical Services Pty Ltd does not routinely store inspection data on company servers.

You are responsible for managing backups and ensuring appropriate data retention practices for your business.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share or disclose your data to third parties except:

  • where required by law;
  • to comply with legal obligations; or
  • where you explicitly choose to export or share data yourself.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to help protect information processed within the App. InspectEquip may provide optional security features such as PIN access control to assist in restricting unauthorised access.

Security of your information also depends on the security of your mobile device and any storage solutions you use. You are responsible for securing your device, enabling device-level passcodes or biometric protection where available, and controlling physical access.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. International Use

InspectEquip may be used internationally. By using the App, you acknowledge that information you enter may be stored and processed on devices or services located in jurisdictions outside your country of residence.

7. Children’s Privacy

InspectEquip is a professional business tool and is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
